# Creaking House Ambience

- **Sounds:** Boards groaning, wind whistling under eaves, occasional rattling window latch, whispered prayers.
- **Lighting:** Candlelight flicker; shadows stretch across walls like hands.
- **Smell:** Incense overlaid on rot and old sweat.
- **DM Actions:** Tap knuckles on table irregularly; occasionally blow gently across mic to mimic drafts.
- **Mechanical Option:** Any attempt to sleep requires DC 10 Wisdom save or wake once in the night from a nightmare (no exhaustion, just mood).
