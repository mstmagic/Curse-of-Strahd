# Mist & Chill Soundscape

- **Temperature:** Just above freezing; breath visible.
- **Sound Cues:** Distant wolf howl every 10â€“15 minutes, raven caws near, muffled bell, wind through pines.
- **Smell:** Wet earth, mildew, faint iron.
- **Lighting:** Diffuse gray; shadows blur at the edges.
- **DM Actions:** Rub hands together, shiver visibly. Lower voice volume slightly as if dampened by fog.
- **Mechanical Option:** Apply -2 to Passive Perception for non-raven PCs; tracks are muddied increasing Survival DCs by 2.

## Use Cases
- Road travel, early mornings, graveyard scenes, Strahd surveillance pass.
