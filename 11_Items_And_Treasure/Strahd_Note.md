# Strahd's Mocking Note

- **Appearance:** Thick parchment, black wax seal with raven skull motif.
- **Text (read slowly):**
  > “To my unexpected guests—your courage is refreshing. I wonder if it will taste the same.
  > Do keep her safe; I am eager to see how long your light lasts.
  > —S.”
- **Use:** Leave after early encounters to taunt. Grants 1 inspiration to any PC who loudly mocks the note in character.
